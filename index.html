Supertree Grove

These are some very large trees!

Measuring between 25 and 50 metres tall, Gardens by the Bay's Supertrees are designed with large canopies that provide shade in the day and come alive with an exhilarating display of lights and sounds at night.

Click on the bottom images to learn more about what you can do at the Supertree Grove!


Garden Rapsody


With 2 shows per day, get lost in beautiful musical soundtracks and stunning choreography of lights.

OCBD Skyway


This 128-metre long aerial walkway gives visitors glorious photo-taking opportunities to capture the scenic beauty of the surrounding flora.

Observatory


Located at the canopy of the tallest Supertree at Supertree Grove, visitors can soak in unblocked cityscape views at this open-air rooftop deck.

Cloud Forest

A mysterious world veiled in the clouds...(Actually it's just mist).

Explore the tropical highlands amidst orchids, pitcher plants and ferns from the cool-moist Tropical Montane region. Be amazed as you encounter our breath-taking indoor waterfall at 30 metres and descend through the surreal mist-filled Cloud Walk and Treetop Walk.

Click on the images to learn more about these features!


Featured Sculptures

In collaboration with Taiwan’s Chini Gallery, we are presenting the first full-scale art exhibition in Cloud Forest, Lee Kuang-Yu's “A Sculptor’s Secret Garden”. This 16-piece art exhibition is curated by Tan Hwee Koon.

Waterfall View

While enjoying the cooling path behind the waterfall, take a moment to consider the importance of earth’s sources of fresh water. Learn how cloud forests capture water droplets from mist and fog by means of epiphyte-laden branches.

Cloud Forest Theatre

Moving on from Cloud Forest Gallery, find out how we as individuals can do our part to help conserve nature, veer away from potential disaster, and move towards a more sustainable future.

Flower Dome

So many flowers!

Enter a world of perpetual spring! Replicating the cool and dry Mediterranean climate, Flower Dome showcases exotic plants from five continents which are displayed in nine different Gardens.

The Flower Dome is filled with various events round the year. This time, Hello Kitty is coming to the Flower Dome!
